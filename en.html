Al-Rawan Complex App

A comprehensive and easy-to-use tool to manage all aspects of life within the residential complex.

App Features

Owner Account

Each unit owner in the complex gets a unique account to view details of their unit, generate invoices, and get reminders for due payments.

Customer Profile

Customers can update their profile picture, view unit payments, and even submit complaints directly through the app.

Monthly Service Bills

The app allows users to view monthly service bills for security, cleaning, water, gas, and internet services.

Maintenance Requests

The app provides the option to request maintenance services directly, with facial recognition to avoid unwanted requests by children.

Guest QR Code Sharing

Residents can share a unique QR code for guests, which is verified by security upon arrival. This code is valid for a single use.

Guard Account

A dedicated account for security guards to capture guest QR codes.

Complex Notifications

The app sends notifications about the latest news and available sales offers within the complex.

Sales Management

The app allows potential buyers to reserve units by filling out personal information and sending it to the sales team.
